History

Mandav is situated 2000 feet above sea level on the Vindhyachal Mountain. It may be a little city today, but a millennium ago it was among the biggest cities on Earth.

Mandu is a monument to the life, happiness, and love that poet-prince Baz Bahadur had for his stunning consort, Rani Roopmati. Baz Bahadur's Palace, a stunning example of Afghan architecture, is still visible from Roopmati's Pavilion, perched atop a hill, while the balladeers of Malwa continue to sing of the romance between these royal lovers.
